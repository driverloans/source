Reinventing Logbook Loans

An online logbook loan by driver loans is similar to a conventional logbook loan however it is more convinient and affordable. Unlike conventional logbook lenders we do not have high overheads from maintaining high street shops and paying sales agents and we pass the savings on to you. Our secured loans are 100th of the APR of a conventional logbook loan.
